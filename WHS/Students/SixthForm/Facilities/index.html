Whitchurch Sixth Form Centre is proud of the many facilities that it can offer its students.

These facilities include:

- Extensive sports facilities

- Several State of the Art Computer Suites

- Modern Science Laboratories

- Art Exhibition

- Drama Studio

- New Music Facilities

- Quiet Study Areas

- Library with online service

- Large Common Room

- Cafeteria with hot and cold options

- Free Wi-Fi

Lower School is where a small proportion of lessons take place for specific subject areas, which include Art, Drama, Food, Music, and some Physical Education lessons.
