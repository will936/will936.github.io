As a prospective student you are about to make decisions about your future that will be formed by your choices - for the first time you will no longer be in compulsory education.

In deciding to “stay-on” into the Sixth Form you will no doubt have spent time in serious thought.

Many of you will have a specific career in mind but all of you will see Sixth Form study as a means of providing you with qualifications, experience and opportunities.

This site will provide you with the subject information that you will need to make informed choices.

There are numerous qualifications available at Whitchurch Sixth Form Centre, for more information on the different courses click on the relevant qualifications.

When you have looked at the different qualifications and decided on what you would like to do in the sixth form complete our Application Form and sign the Student Contract.
