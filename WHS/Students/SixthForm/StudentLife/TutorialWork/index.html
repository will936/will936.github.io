Subject tutors will ensure that all students are well prepared in respect of academic studies but there are other important issues that school and student must address:

- Preparation for entrance to University and Higher Education.

- Preparation for the world of work by gaining valued and valuable “work experience."

- Maintaining contact with skills already acquired.

- Broadening skills and moreover acquiring new skills.

Each student in Years 12 and 13 will meet with their form teacher in a tutorial setting. This is a vital contact in monitoring student progress and in the preparation for Higher Education. Students not attending morning tutorials without a valid reason face the possibility of being asked to leave the sixth form.
