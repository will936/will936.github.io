Admissions

Whitchurch High School is a popular co-educational maintained comprehensive school with almost 2,400 pupils on roll. The school is based on two sites in the suburb of Whitchurch. Years 7, 8 and 9 are based on the lower school site off Manor Way, with Years 10, 11, 12 and 13 students on the upper school site in the Village.

We are an inclusive school and have a SEN Specialist Resource Base (SRB) on both sites which admits up to seventy pupils from throughout the city. Cardiff Council separately administers all admissions for the SRB in partnership with the school.

As a Foundation School the governors are the Admissions Authority and therefore directly manage all mainstream admissions.

Sixth Form

The following link will take you to the log on screen for the sixth form application form and associated documents: https://whitchurchhighschool.applicaa.com/sixthform

The Sixth Form option grid is available here. Therefore, courses cannot be selected on the application form until after this date. The deadline for the initial round of applications is 28th February 2022.

Internal students will receive a username and temporary password via their school email during the week beginning 7th February 2022 (please check the junk email folder if it hasn’t arrived).  This will allow students to log in to access the application form which will have a number of sections already completed with information we hold on SIMS.  Course choices and some additional information will then need to be added.  When completing their application, students will be asked to read two documents, the first runs through the sixth form admissions requirements and the second is the sixth form student contract.  They will then need to sign to confirm that they have read, understood and agree with the content of each document.  A parent or carer will also need to read both documents and sign to confirm that they have read, understood and agree with the content of each document.  Copies of both documents can be seen below.

External students will need to follow the link above to access the sixth form application page and select start new application.  Basic information will need to be added to complete the initial registration.  An email will then be sent to the address provided with a verification link (please check the junk email folder if it hasn’t arrived).  Once the email has been validated, the application form can be completed.  When completing their application, students will be asked to read two documents, the first runs through the sixth form admissions requirements and the second is the sixth form student contract.  They will then need to sign to confirm that they have read, understood and agree with the content of each document.  A parent or carer will also need to read both documents and sign to confirm that they have read, understood and agree with the content of each document.  Copies of both documents can be seen below.

Specialist Resource Base

Students are identified through the Annual Review Process in their Primary schools usually during year 5 and if the SRB at Whitchurch High school is a consideration, then parents are invited to visit.

Please note that staff at Whitchurch High school cannot decide who will be allowed a place.

This decision is made by the Case Advisory Panel, following all advice received during the student’s Annual Review process.

When the Statement is amended to show Whitchurch High school SRB as the placement for the student in year 7, then a Transition programme will be implemented. This will begin in the Summer Term in year 6.
