Science is the subject concerned with learning about the world in which we live and the universe beyond. Science aims to give a balance between the acquisition of scientific knowledge and the practice of scientific method.

Leisure, entertainment, medicine, communications. In the past ten years these have changed dramatically. By studying Electronics, we find out how this progress has been possible, and what changes might arise in the next decade.

Electronics is a valuable, vibrant component of the local and national economy. Young women and men who study GCSE and A level Electronics at Whitchurch High School gain unique skills as they move onto the next stage of their education.
