Opening of the Music Centre - 2009

  • back to Music Department Homepage

 

On Wednesday 21st October 2009, we were delighted to open our new music centre, which is named after Mr Gareth Matthewson OBE, former headteacher of Whitchurch High School 1994 – 2008.

The opening of this magnificent building will allow many generations of current and future Whitchurch students to further develop their musical interests.

Mr Eric Phillips, then Head of Music, started the proceedings, 'It is with great pleasure to welcome our sixty guests, including governors, former members of staff and many of the contractors who had been involved in the building of our wonderful new facility, to school for the official opening of the new Music Centre'.
