Whitchurch High School's 'Wall of Fame' celebrates those the boy and girl students who achieve elite sporting success at school level and beyond over many years. The photographs are all of the same size to give equity of celebration.

See the photographs above showing the WHS 'Wall of Fame'. These do not include framed shirts which are displayed in the Sports Hall.

The photographs towards the end feature Geraint Thomas. In celebration of Geraint's 2018 Tour de France win we have lined the framing battens in gold coloured braid as a symbolic mark of celebration.
