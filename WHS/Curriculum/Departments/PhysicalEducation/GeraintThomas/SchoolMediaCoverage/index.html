Whitchurch High School is proud that Geraint Thomas is a former student of the school. Geraint’s achievement in wining the 2018 Tour de France is incredible.

The world interest in Geraint's 2018 Tour de France win has meant that the school has been inundated by numerous requests from UK and overseas TV, radio, newspapers and magazine for interviews and visits. The school has tried to accommodate these despite the holiday closure period.
