Geraint’s historic win of the Tour de France is celebrated with absolute delight by the whole school community, even though it happened when the school has broken up for the summer holidays.

It is amazing to see that one of our former students has won the Tour de France and become the first Welshman to do so.

Geraint is already a legend at the school. Being a triple World Champion, double Olympic gold medallist and Commonwealth gold medallist amongst his many incredible achievements. Now he can add being a Tour de France winner!

The last few stages were nerve wracking for us all but we are so proud and congratulate Geraint on topping the podium in Paris, as the colossus he is.  

We applaud Geraint’s talent and are inspired by his resilience, inner confidence and modesty which has seen him deliver a master class of peak performance. These are life skill qualities we try to develop in every student at Whitchurch High School, in all areas of school life. We ensure that self-belief and worth are nurtured and founded on hard work and a positive outlook. Students are encouraged to 'Dream Big’.  

Geraint’s Tour de France win has secured his place amongst the World sporting elite of any era and surely must lay claim to him being Wales' most successful sporting superstar.
