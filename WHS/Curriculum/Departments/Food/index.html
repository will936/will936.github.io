Home Economics

back to Departments Homepage

We are fortunate as a department that we are able to teach Home Economics to the pupils at the school and therefore offer more opportunity for practical work.

All pupils in Lower School will participate in Home Economics lessons, once a week for two terms in each year group.

Pupils will be taught about food through both theory and practical lessons, and will have the opportunity to cook in school about 5 times each term. We believe as a department that pupils should be given information about the links between their diet and health that will enable them to make healthy choices themselves.

The topics that are taught within Home Economics will hopefully provide information and skills to the pupils that will be useful to them for life.

Courses on offer in the HE department:

GCSE
  • GCSE Food & Nutrition
  • GCSE Hospitality & Catering
  • GCSE Health, Social Care and Childcare

If you require further information, please contact Mrs K Dyson, Departmental Leader.
