Eisteddfod

Our year 7 pupils celebrate our annual Eisteddfod. Students, representing three houses, Coch, Gwyn a Gwyrdd, take part in a wide variety of stage competitions from solo singing, dancing and drama to recitation and instrumentals.

There is a high standard throughout the day always with some unforgettable performances. In addition, fourteen departments taken part in off stage competitions where some outstanding work is produced.
